Who I Am.

To most people that probably means I spend hours learning the ins and outs of coding languages, spend more time talking to my computer than I do to other people, and my mornings begin with a Mountain Dew Kickstart because sometimes I sleep a lot less than I probably should. While these are partially true, above all else my computer is the way I give. It's my way to give to people experiences, information, laughs, energy, community, and happiness.
